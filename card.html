Tiger

Very good swimmers and have been known to kill prey while swimming.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris
  • Speed: 40 mph
  • Average Lifespan: 26 years
  • Habitat: Forests, Grasslands and Savannah

The tiger is the largest cat species, most recognizable for their pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside. They are territorial and generally solitary but social animals, often requiring large contiguous areas of habitat that support their prey requirements.
